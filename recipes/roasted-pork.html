Roasted Pork

About this recipe

This crunchy, yet pull apart pork recipe will delight your family

It's quick and easy to make. Great for a weeknight dinner, since it requires little prep work.

Ingredients

  • 600g pork loin
  • honey mustard, enough to massage the whole piece of meat
  • ghee
  • salt

Steps

  1. Grease an oven safe dish with the ghee.
  2. Pierce the meat in multiple places with a fork.
  3. Massage the honey mustard into the meat and season with salt.
  4. Place the meat in the baking dish and place a few blobs of ghee on top of the meat.
  5. Cover the baking dish with aluminium foil and pierce a few holes with a toothpick.
  6. Cook in a preheated oven, 180 Celsius, for about two hour, or until it starts to shred.
